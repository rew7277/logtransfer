FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes — you can start on the Starter plan with no credit card required. The Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial when you sign up with a workspace email.
What counts as a "log event"? ▼
Each line ingested via the REST API, file upload, S3 job, or webhook counts as one event. Structured JSON objects count as one event regardless of payload size.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? ▼
Yes. You can upgrade instantly and get prorated credit. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle — no data is deleted immediately.
Do you support on-prem deployment? ▼
On-premises and private cloud deployments are available under the Enterprise plan. Contact sales@observex.in for a custom quote and deployment guide.
Which payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and net banking via Razorpay. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice (NEFT / IMPS).
Is my data safe? ▼
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never sell or share your log data. Enterprise plans include dedicated data residency options.
