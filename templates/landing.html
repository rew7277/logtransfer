FAQ

Common implementation questions

Clear answers help users trust the product and understand what is public, what is protected, and what needs deployment setup.

Can users access the workspace without sign in?

No. The landing site, pricing page, login page, create-account page, and demo form stay public. The workspace and operational APIs require authentication.

Can users still upload local log files?

Yes. The upload action is part of the main workspace flow and stays visible in overview and log explorer.

Do org subdomains show automatically?

Not until real wildcard DNS and Railway custom domain routing are configured. The app stores the org slug so it is subdomain-ready.
