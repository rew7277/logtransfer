How it works

Upload or connect

2

Search & analyse

Filter by level, source, or keyword in the log explorer. View level-mix charts and top-source breakdowns on the overview dashboard.

3

Alert & collaborate

Set alert rules for critical thresholds. Save useful views as dashboards. Invite your team and assign appropriate roles.
