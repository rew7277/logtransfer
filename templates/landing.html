FAQ

Common questions

Can users access the workspace without sign in?

No. The public site, pricing page, sign-in page, create-account page, and demo form stay public. The workspace and all operational APIs require login.

Why do subdomains not show automatically?

The app stores organization slug in code and data, but real tenant URLs need Railway custom domains plus wildcard DNS.

Can I still upload local logs?

Yes. Manual file upload stays available inside the workspace overview and log explorer.
